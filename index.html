About us

The Candy X_Perience is a platform that the youth from all walks of life meet and talk about everything they are going through.We encourage everyone to share the joys and their pains and anything that is overwhelming them so that they can feel better and also encourage others who are also going through the same. We also give advice to anyone who needs it and encourage everyone to interact and share their ideas on some interactive discussions we have on a daily basis.We get to have experts from all career fields to answer questions that we as the youth always ask. We believe that this is a platform where we find peace.

We talk about:

  • Family
  • Relationships
  • Mental health
  • Career
